Who we are

The Company Has Enlarged Its Size And Technology Using Latest Machinery In Wood Work And Joinery Under The Supervision Of Professionals Utilizing To Increase Production And Efficiency. Kanaan Decor Is A Modern And Highly Efficient Company. We Manufacture Everything From Wooden Doors, To Wardrobes And Furniture And Each Are Inspected For Perfection.

To This Day, The Company Still Manufactures And Distributes To All Corners In UAE And Arab Countries. At A Time When Many Manufacturers Are Struggling To Find Their Ways Through Difficult Times, Kanaan Décor Strategic Direction And Achievements Are Clear. Its Management Targets Are To Assure Quality, The Pursuit Of Excellence, To Serve The Customers And To Be In The Top Of UAE Woodwork Manufacturers.
